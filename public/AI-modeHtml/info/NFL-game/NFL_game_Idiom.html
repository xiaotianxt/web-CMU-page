Idiom English Dictionary

NFL game

noun

  1. A professional American football game played as part of the National Football League, an organization that consists of 32 teams divided between the National Football Conference (NFC) and the American Football Conference (AFC).

    Example

    The NFL game on Sunday attracted millions of viewers across the country.

    Synonyms

    American football game, professional football game

deconstruction

  • NFL + game
